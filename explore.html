Agriculture Equipments

Tractor

A versatile farming vehicle used for plowing, planting, and other agricultural tasks.

Harvester

Automated machinery used for harvesting crops such as wheat, corn, and soybeans.

Plow

Instrument used for breaking, lifting, and turning soil for planting crops.

Sprayer

Device for applying pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers to crops.

Seeder

Machine used to plant seeds in the soil at precise intervals and depths.

Combine Harvester

Integrated machine for reaping, threshing, and winnowing grain crops.

Cultivator

Implements for stirring and pulverizing the soil, either before planting or to remove weeds.

Irrigation System

System for supplying water to agricultural land to promote growth.

Tractor Trailer

Attachment for a tractor used to transport goods and materials in agriculture.

Mower

Machine for cutting and trimming grass or other plants on the farm.
